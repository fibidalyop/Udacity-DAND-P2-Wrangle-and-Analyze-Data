Insights:¶

After gathering, wrangling, cleaning and assessing my three datasets they following insights were deduced:

  1. The labrador_retriever which has an average #1 prediction confidence of 0.825333 and an average numerator rating of 13 has the highest retweet count average, which tells us that the labrador_retiebver breed may be among the most favorite dog breeds.

  2. The irish_terrier which has an aveage #3 predicition confidence of 0.069126 has the highest favourite_count average. This suggest that it may also be among the most favorite dog breeds

  3. Other favourite breeds may include the chihuahua, english_springer,standard_poodle among others

  4. There is a strong positive correlation betwenen retweet_count and favoirite count which is expected, as most people who retweet a tweet, will most likely tag it as favourite

  5. The data source came mostly from Twitter users using Iphone, while tweet deck had the least

  6. Dogs categorized as both doggo and puppos stages have the highest avearge retweet count, suggesting that, that may be the sample's favourite stage. Dogs categorized under the pupper stage have the lowest average retweet count

  7. Dogs in Doggo and pupper stage has the highest average rating, while dogs in both doggo and floofer have the lowest avearge rating

  8. The p1_dog and p2_dog (dog predictions) that are "True" have higher average confidence than the p1_dog and p2_dog that are "False". However the p3_dog prediction that are "False" have a slightly higher average confidence that those that are "True"
